monthly_sales = df.groupby('Month')['TotalPrice'].sum()
monthly_sales.plot(kind='bar', title='Monthly Revenue', color='skyblue')
plt.xlabel("Month")
plt.ylabel("Revenue")
plt.show()

Business Insight: Monthly Sales Trends

  • The highest sales occurred in November, suggesting a potential seasonal trend (holiday sales, bulk ordering).
  • Months like February and August saw lower revenue. The company could explore promotions during these months to balance sales.

Recommendation: Consider ramping up inventory and marketing before November to capture high demand efficiently.

Final Summary & Recommendations

Revenue Trends:

  • Peak month is November prep for holiday sales.
  • Down months like August target marketing & promotions.

Top Products:

  • Focus on bundling best-sellers.
  • Review low-movement items and reassess inventory.

Top Countries:

  • UK is the core market.
  • Explore growth in top 3 non-UK countries.

Customer Insights:

  • High concentration of revenue from a few customers retention is key.
  • Create loyalty programs or referral incentives.

Outliers:

  • Very large orders found likely wholesale/B2B.
